Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

He's music save the world

Here's a time line of the musician Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

  • 1762: Mozart begins performing in public as a child prodigy
  • 1763: Mozart and his family go on their first European tour
  • 1768: Mozart writes his first opera, "La finta semplice"
  • 1777: Mozart is appointed as a court musician in Salzburg by the Archbishop of Salzburg
  • 1781: Mozart's opera "Idomeneo" is a success in Munich
  • 1786: Mozart's opera "The Marriage of Figaro" premieres in Vienna
  • 1787: Mozart's opera "Don Giovanni" premieres in Prague
  • 1789: Mozart becomes the court composer for Emperor Joseph II in Vienna
  • 1790: Mozart writes his last opera, "The Magic Flute"
  • 1791: Mozart completes his famous "Requiem" shortly before his death
  • 1791: Mozart completes his clarinet concerto just two months before his death.
