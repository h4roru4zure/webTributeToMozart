Here's a time line of the musician Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

  1. 1762: Mozart begins performing in public as a child prodigy
  2. 1763: Mozart and his family go on their first European tour
  3. 1768: Mozart writes his first opera, "La finta semplice"
  4. 1781: Mozart's opera "Idomeneo" is a success in Munich
  5. 1786: Mozart's opera "The Marriage of Figaro" premieres in Vienna
  6. 1790: Mozart writes his last opera, "The Magic Flute"
